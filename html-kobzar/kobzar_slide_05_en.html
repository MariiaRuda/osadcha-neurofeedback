What is currently missing
05 / 18

Neurofeedback remains largely unavailable, and the research base — insufficient.

Modern infrastructure is needed: EEG equipment, software, and local research for military personnel.

Potential impact of the research

For patients
  • improved attention and memory;
  • reduced anxiety and tension;
  • stabilization of emotional state;
  • improved sleep;
  • easier adaptation after combat experience.
For the rehabilitation system
  • introduction of modern neurorehabilitation methods;
  • development of evidence-based support for military personnel;
  • development of new rehabilitation protocols.
For science and international collaboration
  • creation of a clinical database;
  • opportunities for international research;
  • advancement of neurofeedback research in Ukraine.
